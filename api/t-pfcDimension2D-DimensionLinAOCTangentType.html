Enum pfcDimensionLinAOCTangentType

# include <pfcDimension2D.h>

enum pfcDimensionLinAOCTangentType
{
    pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_LEFT0,
    pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_RIGHT0,
    pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_LEFT1,
    pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_RIGHT1,
    pfcDimensionLinAOCTangentType_nil
};

Description

This enumerated type contains the types of references used by a pfcLinAOCTangentDimensionSense object.
See Also:
pfcLinAOCTangentDimensionSense, pfcDimension2D::GetDimensionSenses(), pfcLinAOCTangentDimensionSense::Create(pfcDimensionLinAOCTangentType)



Values

pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_LEFT0
The tangent is to the left of the line, and on the same side of the center of the arc/circle as the line.
pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_RIGHT0
The tangent is to the right of the line, and on the same side of the center of the arc/circle as the line.
pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_LEFT1
The tangent is to the left of the line, and on the opposite sid of the center of the arc/circle to the line.
pfcDIMLINAOCTANGENT_RIGHT1
The tangent is to the right of the line, and on the opposite side of the center of the arc/circle to the line.
pfcDimensionLinAOCTangentType_nil
Enum null value.
